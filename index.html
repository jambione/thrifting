thrifting over 50

Thrifting isn’t just for twenty-somethings.

I turned 50 at the end of 2025. I’d lost a lot of weight, and realized that I had no idea how to dress for my age. Since the scale wasn’t constantly fluctuating but my wallet wasn’t getting any bigger, I decided thrifting was the best way to keep my closet fresh. I created this blog to document my style journey!
